SBI Q3 FY26 Results: Net Profit Surges 24.5% to 21,028 Crore - Asset Quality Improves Significantly
https://lnkd.in/gzkum7xA
State Bank of India, India's largest lender, delivered outstanding quarterly results with strong profit growth, improved margins, and remarkable asset quality enhancement.

Q3 FY26 Standalone Key Figures:

 Net Profit: 21,028 crore (Up 24.5% YoY)
 Net Interest Income: 41,497 crore (Up 5.4% YoY)
 Net Interest Margin: 3.36% (Healthy)
 Operating Profit: 32,862 crore (Up 16.6% YoY)

Asset Quality (Major Improvement):

 Gross NPA: 2.15% (vs 2.42% YoY)
 Net NPA: 0.39% (vs 0.53% YoY)
 Provision Coverage Ratio: 75.54%
 Slippage Ratio: Controlled at 0.28%

Capital Strength:

 CRAR: 14.05% (Strong)
 CET-1 Ratio: 10.99%
 Return on Assets: 1.19% (annualized)
 EPS: 22.78 for the quarter

9M FY26 Performance:

 Net Profit: 60,348 crore
 NII: 1,25,117 crore
 Operating Profit: 90,718 crore

Consolidated Performance:

 Q3 Net Profit: 21,317 crore (Up 21.1% YoY)
 9M Net Profit: 63,656 crore

Key Corporate Actions:

1. YES Bank Divestment: Sold 13.18% stake, recorded 3,026 crore exceptional profit
2. SBI General Insurance: Increased stake to 73.89%
3. FPO Success: Raised 25,000 crore in July 2025
4. Dividend History: Consistent shareholder returns

Business Growth Drivers:

 Credit Growth: Strong across retail, corporate segments
 Deposit Growth: Stable CASA ratio maintained
 Digital Banking: YONO platform continues to gain traction
 Fee Income: Robust growth in non-interest income

Stock Performance:

 Share price: 1,066.40 (Down 0.66%)
 Trading with positive long-term momentum
 Attractive dividend yield expected

Sector Outlook:

1. Rate Cycle Benefits: NIMs to remain stable
2. Economic Recovery: Credit demand picking up
3. Asset Quality: Continued improvement trajectory
4. Market Share: Maintaining leadership position

Why SBI Stands Out:

1. Scale Advantage: Unmatched branch network
2. Digital Leadership: YONO with 60+ million users
3. Subsidiary Value: Insurance, AMC, Cards businesses
4. Government Backing: Strategic importance
