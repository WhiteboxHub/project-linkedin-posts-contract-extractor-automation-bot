The job markets a knife fight right now, and pretending it's not us a surefire way to lose.

If youre out of work, blasting applications is just screaming into the void; the only move that matters is positioning yourself close to revenue or real impact and proving it fast.

If youre still employed, youre not exactly safe. 

Many of my W2 brethren have told me they could never be entrepreneurs because they care too much about paychecks, and theyre not wrong. Ive gone long stretches with zero income, paid employees while paying myself nothing, and lived with uncertainty most people would tap out from. 

But heres the part thats changed: the paycheck guarantee isn't the same as it used to be.

And AI? AI should be doing the boring stuff while you show judgment, taste, and restraint, because the interchangeable work gets cut first.
